How to Use the Profit Loss Calculator

Input Fields

  • Cost Price (CP):
    • Enter the purchase or manufacturing cost
    • Use numbers only, without currency symbols
  • Selling Price (SP):
    • Enter the amount you sold or plan to sell for
    • Use numbers only, without currency symbols

Results Explained

  • Profit/Loss Amount: The absolute difference between SP and CP
  • Profit/Loss Percentage: The percentage gain or loss on the investment
  • Color Coding:
    • Green: Indicates a profit (SP > CP)
    • Red: Indicates a loss (SP < CP)
    • Gray: No profit/loss (SP = CP)

Tips

  • Click Calculate to see the results
  • Use the Reset button to clear all inputs
  • Results are rounded to 2 decimal places
  • The calculator handles both profit and loss scenarios automatically
